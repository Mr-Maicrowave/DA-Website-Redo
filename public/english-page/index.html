The senior lesson pathway

A premium roadmap replaces the bulky step boxes. It still feels structured, but the staggered path gives the section more movement and polish.

1

Rubric Deconstruction

Students unpack the module rubric so they understand what the syllabus requires.

2

Textual Analysis

They learn to notice technique, purpose and meaning, then connect evidence to argument.

3

Guided Response Building

Ideas become paragraphs with thesis, topic sentence, evidence and commentary working together.

4

Draft Development

Students refine expression, structure and depth before moving into timed performance.

5

Personalised Feedback

Corrections are specific enough that students know what to fix next.

6

Refinement and Exam Prep

Students practise adapting their strongest ideas under real exam conditions.
