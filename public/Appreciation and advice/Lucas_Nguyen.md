## Appreciation

I have found that a deep connection with the material taught by the teachers at DA have led to my academic success. Notably, the guidance and expertise of tutors have been pivotal in developing my academic ability. A prime example is my work in mathematics under the mentorship of both Miss Amanda and Miss Linda. I have been able to cultivate deep insights into complex mathematical concepts through their “gangsta method”. Her tailored approach, which emphasises problem-solving techniques and the application of abstract principles, has enabled me to approach challenges with clarity and confidence.  

Similarly, Miss Lai’s instruction in English has been instrumental in enhancing my analytical and literary skills. Her emphasis on critical analysis of complex techniques, structured writing, and nuanced interpretation has not only sharpened my comprehension but also refined my ability to articulate ideas coherently and persuasively. Both tutors’ attention to detail has made this a place where I can excel academically and actually develop a love for learning.

## Advice for current students

I encourage fellow peers to take up the recommendations of our tutorial. Their guidance has personally allowed me to develop discipline in school and my studies; furthermore the enemy of success is procrastination, so one should ensure to devote time to each subject, not to memorise superficially but to understand deeply. On a similar note, I wish you would look for extra “help class” support to consolidate what I acquire in class.  

My academic experience with Miss Amanda and Miss Lai underscores the potential that personalised direct instruction may have on one’s academic excellence. Their tailored feedback and attentive guidance have been essential in both mitigating my weaknesses and whittling down my strengths for better service. It is also important to accept the process of making mistakes because it is often the most instructive moments. Mistakes are inevitable in the learning process and are imperative in one’s growth academically.  

Finally, the development of genuine curiosity and love for the subject turns academic work from a duty into an intellectually enriching process. When learning becomes a pursuit of knowledge rather than a task to be completed, the path to excellence becomes significantly more attainable. And hence, I hope my academic excellence can serve as motivation for those reading these lines.
