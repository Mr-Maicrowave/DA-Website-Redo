# Marcus Nguyen

## Appreciation

The main factor that contributed to my success in school was learning to be competitive and study more than everyone else. The DA tutors really helped me out with this by providing all the resources I needed and keeping me accountable for my work and my marks.

## Advice for current students

The main advice I would have to give to another student wanting to improve but not having the drive or motivation to do so is to learn to love the subject you do at school. I used to hate doing maths and found it tedious to do, but DA has broadened my mindset and made me appreciate and enjoy the work I was studying. This made it so much easier to improve and understand the work which I believe ultimately contributed to my success in school.
