## Appreciation

The first factor which helped me to do well is my own desire and resolve. Often I would be tempted to procrastinate but I was always reminded of how important it is to me to maintain my grades. It is understandable that this discipline is not easy for everyone to attain. However this personal restraint allowed me to do my best.  

The second factor, possibly the most important for me, is the support I received from others whether it be family, friends or tutors. I feel extremely grateful for my mum who always encourages me to do my best. Additionally, all the kind tutors have done so much for me, such as taking time outside of class to clarify any concerns that I may have and also teaching more than just the subject like life skills to set me up for success.  

Finally the last factor is my ability to move on from failures. This final factor is an important lesson which Miss Amanda left lingering in my mind. I’m not talking about treating your mistakes indifferently and forgetting about them. While it is important to recover from a failure with a positive mindset, you should still recall mistakes and learn from them. These mistakes whether small and silly or not, I would often be harsh on myself making sure to not repeat them.

## Advice for current students

To achieve the same goal, I suggest you take advantage of all your resources. As a student of DA it is incredibly easy to get the right resources to supplement your weaknesses. It’s just a matter of taking some free time to complete extra work to boost your confidence when it comes to exam-style questions. Moreover, a main obstacle for many students is the way they perceive themselves. Many students are daunted by the fact they are not as talented or as capable as others, and as a result they give up. Not as in giving up studying but rather giving up on surpassing or matching their peers. Personally I believe the hard work and effort you put in outweighs talent. So my final piece of advice is to stay hard working and to keep reminding yourself of your goal.
