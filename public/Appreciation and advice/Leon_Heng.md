## Appreciation

The main factors which have contributed to helping me come first in the grade for so many school exams is mainly due to time management. Time management has allowed me to avoid stress, completing assessments early giving more time to study and improve when the next assessment arrives. Another factor is consistently doing homework with full working out with solutions. This allows me to recite all my formulas and techniques to avoid any mistakes in the future.

## Advice for current students

Tips I would share to other kids who want to achieve the same goal as me is to put in the time and effort to improve as even 30 minutes a day can improve drastically and to manage any work needed to make studying more smooth and effective.
