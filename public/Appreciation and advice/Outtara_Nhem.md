## Appreciation

Firstly, I think the main factors that helped me was my passion for maths. There is nothing that can overcome this factor. If you don’t enjoy the subjects you are taking, then you are limiting yourself. I think by liking the subject, instead of thinking of homeworks as something you need to do just for the sake of it, it becomes something you would like to do. This thrill really comes from topics you find particularly difficult but through the hard work you put in, you will start to see improvements and think to yourself “omg! I never thought I’d be able to do this” and I would say this mindset of always trying to see improvements in areas I’m weak in helped me. Moreover, another factor that helped me is definitely tutoring. My tutor explained each topic very clearly which helped me understand and apply the concepts taught easily. This combined with the constant push for success really helped me attain all those high marks.

## Advice for current students

I would say you need to try to understand all the topics at the time they are taught. You shouldn’t have the mentality of skipping topics that you find difficult and saying that you’ll come back to it later because you won’t have time. If you find it extremely difficult, I would recommend going tutoring as all topics in year 11 and 12 are tested in the HSC, so it is essential you understand each topic fully. Moreover, since most topics are linked up, if you don’t understand one, it might affect your understanding of another topic and so on and thus, would really be detrimental to your whole understanding of the subject.
