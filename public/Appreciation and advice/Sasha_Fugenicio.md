# Sasha Fugenicio

## Appreciation

I believe what helped me consistently achieve top marks in all of my school exams stemmed from the genuine interest and appreciation I had for each of my subjects which ultimately fueled my willingness to engage with content and succeed. By developing study habits and understanding how to balance each subject depending on how confident I was with my abilities in them was also crucial in determining academic success. Of course, my tutors played a pivotal role in helping achieve first place in exams, not only encouraging me to push beyond my perceived limits but also illuminating concepts in different lights allowing me to better understand the content. Through their unrelenting support and unique insights I was able to consistently stay at the top during my exams.

## Advice for current students

The key advice I’d share with others aiming to achieve the highest rank in every exam is to understand that reaching the top is challenging, but maintaining that position requires even greater effort. Realising this is what pushed me the most to try harder each assessment and stay disciplined throughout my HSC year. Also, engaging in class, both at school and during tutoring sessions, is essential as I believe that was another factor that lead to my academic success. By asking questions and seeking clarity I was able to deepen my understanding of my courses and solidify knowledge. Consistent effort, a proactive mindset, and a genuine desire to be the best is what cultivates success.
