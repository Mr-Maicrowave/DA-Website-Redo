## Appreciation

A lot of practice. To gain confidence, I would practice the various kinds of questions they might ask on tests. As a result, I could respond to it faster during the test and would know instantly what approach would work best. It has strengthened my comprehension of the subject and given me more self-assurance when taking tests.  

Constantly getting feedback. To get feedback, I would routinely turn in or show my work to my teachers and tutors. The critique can take the form of correcting a mistake or offering pointers and advice that helped me identify an appropriate approach to problem-solving. I would also apply this advice for my own work.  

Past exams. I usually just go over my past exams again and review the problems that were very difficult and the ones that I answered wrong. In order to structure my answers for real exams and receive the most points possible, I would additionally go through past tests and examine sample solutions.

## Advice for current students

Make use of as many of the materials that DA has to offer. For every topic, DA offers a wide range of resources. Past exams, flash cards, booklets (for certain subjects), sample essays, and much more are included. Additionally, don’t be scared to ask questions of your teachers at school and at DA. I would occasionally be nervous to question my teachers for fear of being judged. However, I then saw that I was losing out on important details that might have strengthened my understanding of a particular subject and improved my studies.
