## Advice for current students

My advice towards other kids is to start by understanding your strengths and weak points. Focus on improving your weak points and never be afraid to ask for help from mentors or friends. Creating a study group can make learning more engaging and enjoyable, as you can share ideas, discuss topics, and motivate one another.  

Another advice that is essential, is that you should begin studying earlier than the last minute. This allows you to be able to understand all the material needed without stressing too much, many people don’t get their desired marks because of studying last minute.  

From experience, cramming at the last minute is rarely effective and often leads to unnecessary stress.
