# Alira Liong

## Appreciation

The main factors I believe have greatly contributed to helping me receive first in the grade in school exams are practice and speed. I do as many questions as I can of the material that would help me become better at ones I’m not so confident in and then mark the questions and see where I went wrong to improve on before the exam. When it comes to the exam, I read all questions properly and underline the key things I need to answer. I do these efficiently regarding the mark allocation and usually finish with 30–40 minutes left depending on the subject. With this time, I consistently check over my work and redo the questions which equates to roughly 3–5 times, where accuracy plays a role.

## Advice for current students

Advice and tips I’d like to share with others on how to achieve the same goal would be listen attentively in class, do all the work that is assigned and that the more work you put in the more that is rewarded to you. I used to not be the most enthusiastic when it came to school, especially the exams. However, this was overcome with discipline and hard work which motivated me to do more, with the eagerness to receive more rewards and achieve more goals.
