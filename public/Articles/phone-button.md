Tips to ChaNge

Whether you did or didn’t push the button, how do you take control of what’s limiting your potential?

Turn off your notifications
Notifications exist to tempt you to look at your phone! Conglomerates don’t care if your friend sent you a TikTok or shared you a reel on Instagram and Facebook!
Their goal is to get you on their app and STAY THERE.

Set screen time limits!
Screen time limits may SEEM useless because you can click “Dismiss for 15 minutes” or “Ignore for the day.”
However, for each time you click “Remind me later” and merrily scroll on your feed, remember what’s happening to your mind. Make the right decision.

P.S. Have someone set up a PASSWORD that you don’t know to keep you off for good!

Out of sight, out of mind
A phone should not be at your desk as you study! A phone should not be who you eat your meals with! A phone should not be the one to kiss you goodnight as you go to bed!

When doing things as simple as survival, keep your phone out of your hands—Out of sight.

Reduce your dependence on needing to pick up that phone for a dangerous buzz of dopamine.
