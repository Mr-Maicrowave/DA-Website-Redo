# Growing… 🐝

*Published: August 16, 2025*  *Author: DA Tuition Team*  
 *Category: Mindset*

 

 Just like Nova, the most talented people around you don’t just make mistakes.
 They do not remain trapped in the ‘All or Nothing’ mindset.
 They make an effort to escape, embracing the small setbacks as a chance to learn so that the same mistake won’t happen again.

Believe it or not, this can be YOU!

## How to Escape the ‘All or Nothing’ Mentality
### Practice Self-Compassion

 It’s crucial to be kind to the people around you but don’t forget yourself.

 Allow yourself to make those mistakes without judgement or negative self-talk.

### Shift Your Focus

 It should never be ‘It’s too hard’, ‘I already suck at this’.

 Rather, focus on accepting your mistakes as you practice positive self-talk e.g. ‘With time & hard work, I will be able to achieve this’, ‘I just need more practice’.

The way in which you speak to yourself before proceeding with something alters how your brain will think going into the experience. When you find yourself already telling yourself that you can’t do it, your brain is ready to do so.

### Speak to the People Around You

 A strong support network that recognises the ‘All or Nothing’ mentality is a barrier to success will assist you in detaching from it.

 I promise you that all tutors at DA make mistakes every single day, myself included, and from their personal experience, these mistakes have allowed them to grow into the teachers they are today.

You are not defined by your mistakes.

You are defined by the growth that comes from embracing and learning from them!

### Set Small Goals

 The harsh reality is that 99% of the time, you will never get things right the first go. So, you must aim for progress, not perfection.

 Because at the end of the day,

All the best art comes from colouring outside the lines.
